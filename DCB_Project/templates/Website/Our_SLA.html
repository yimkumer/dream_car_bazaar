Service Level Agreements (SLA)

  1. Introduction:This Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the terms and conditions governing the services provided by DreamCarBazaar.
  2. Scope of Services: DreamCarBazaar agrees to provide online automotive marketplace services, facilitating the buying and selling of vehicles.
  3. Availability:
    • DreamCarBazaar commits to maintaining a minimum uptime for its platform.
    • Downtime exceeding 0.1% will be communicated in advance, and compensations will be provided as per the terms defined in this SLA.
  4. Performance:
    • DreamCarBazaar aims to ensure optimal platform performance, with minimal page loading time.
    • Any performance issues causing significant delays will be addressed promptly.
  5. Customer Support:
    • DreamCarBazaar will provide customer support through email and chat during business hours (9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday).
    • Response times for critical issues will be within 2 hours, and non-critical issues within 3 to 4 business days.
  6. Data Security:
    • DreamCarBazaar undertakes to protect user data and maintain compliance with applicable data protection regulations.
    • Regular security audits and updates will be conducted to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of user information.
  7. Data Backup:
    • DreamCarBazaar will perform regular data backups to prevent data loss.
    • In case of data loss, the platform will be restored to the latest backup available.
  8. Change Management:
    • DreamCarBazaar will communicate any planned changes to the platform in advance.
    • Changes that may impact service availability will be scheduled during low-traffic periods.
  9. Review and Revision:
    • This SLA will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary.
    • Any amendments will be communicated to users in advance.

By agreeing to use DreamCarBazaar's services, users acknowledge and accept the terms outlined in this SLA.
This SLA is subject to change. Users will be notified of any updates.
